### Parallel Hadamard Test
Soichiro Imamura, Synge Todo  
[arXiv:2607.12344](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.12344)

We propose a parallel Hadamard test that combines multiple expectation-value-estimation tasks into a single circuit type. 
The method reduces circuit-level overhead for three classes of workloads and may lower both runtime and financial cost in cloud quantum computing.
